As a student-oriented team, we aim to provide an amiable and enjoyable living to our residents. Feel free to find us if you need any help!

作為一個以學生為中心的團隊，我們旨在為宿生提供一個和藹可親和愉快的舍堂生活。
請隨時找我們幫助！

"ABC" Motto

"ABC"格言

Hall life is a part of university life where students can live, learn and grow happily together. To develop hall spirit, CSK Hall has its "ABC" motto - "Affection, Bonding and Care".

舍堂生活可以說是大學生涯中的重要一環。學生不但可以在舍堂住宿，更可同時在舍堂中學習以及成長。陳瑞球堂的"ABC"格言可以幫助宿生們了解本舍堂的精神。

Affection

Affection．舍堂情意

Students are encouraged to develop a sense of affection for each other through experiencing satisfactory, on-going relationships with their hall-mates. CSK Hall provides residents with a family atmosphere to form a solid foundation for their study and social activities.

陳瑞球堂鼓勵宿生互相建立持續的關係，享受彼此之間的情意。我們提供了一個充滿家庭氣氛的環境，深信這是宿生在這裡學習和社交的堅實基礎。

Bonding

Bonding．關係結連

This is the development of relationships based on mutual respect and acceptance of differences. We support all non-local students in our Hall as a way to embrace diversity. We encourage residents to integrate and participate in all Hall activities so as to build up multi-cultural friendships.

本著「互相尊重」、「求同存異」的精神，陳瑞球堂支援舍堂內的非本地學生融入舍堂生活之中。我們鼓勵不同背景的宿生一同參與舍堂活動，以建立多元文化的友誼。

Care

Care．宿生關懷

This is our care for the needs, well-being, and academic performance of our residents. We support each and every resident to achieve optimum academic performance, and we show our care by distributing fruits and drinks during the revision weeks. Other activities like study tours, "scholarship trips", and mental health talks will also be arranged to enhance broad-based learning.

陳瑞球堂關顧宿生的需要、福利以及學業成就。我們支援每一位同學取得最佳的學業成就。於考試「溫習週」，我們會派發水果及飲品予宿生，以鼓勵他們溫習。我們舉辦過的遊學團、「獎學金團」及精神健康講座等亦有助宿生去擴闊學習視野。


All these aspects in CSK Hall help our residents to take every opportunity to benefit from, and enjoy their university education to its fullest.




陳瑞球堂致力向宿生們提供各種機會，讓宿生能享受大學生涯的豐富之處。



About

The story of Hall 5, Chan Sui Kau Hall.

關於我們

第五座．陳瑞球堂的故事


  • 2000-2004

    Our Bright Beginnings

    2000年 至 2004年

    我們的起點

    In 2000, City University of Hong Kong launched the student resident project. Phase 1 development was completed in 2002, while Hall 5 was officially opened in Sep 2004, phase 2 of the project.

    香港城市大學於2000年開展學生宿舍興建計劃。
    該發展工程項目分四期進行。第一期的宿舍於2002年完成。而陳瑞球堂則在第二期發展時興建，於2004年9月份正式啓用。



  • April 2006

    Naming Ceremony of the Chan Sui Kau Hall (Hall 5)

    2006年 4月

    陳瑞球堂命名典禮 (第五座)

    Thanks to our donor Dr. Chan's donation, Hall 5 is named officially as "Chan Sui Kau Hall" since 12th April 2006.

    為感謝陳瑞球博士的捐資贊助，學生宿舍第五座於2006年4月12日正式命名為「陳瑞球堂」。




  • 2009

    2009年

    Formation of the CSK Hall Scholarship Team

    獎學金團隊的成立

    Each year, the recipients of the Chan Wing Fui Scholarship / SEAL Scholarship of our hall (Around 16 people) will become the members of the CSK Scholarship Team. The Team aims to organize academic-related activities for our residents.

    在每一年，獲得陳永奎獎學金／SEAL獎學金的宿生（約16人）均會成為獎學金團隊的成員。獎學金團隊旨在舉辦一系列有關學術的活動讓宿生參與。






  • May 2016

    2016年 5月

    Volunteer Work in Bangkok

    曼谷義工之旅

    In May 2016, our Management Team organized a study tour to visit Bangkok, Thailand. Social contribution is one of our highlights in this tour. Before the trip, participants organized toys collection campaign / fund rasing in the hall community, which we were able to take those toys and money to an orphanage in Bangkok later in the tour. Furthermore, we also successfully collected tons of can rings from the hall community as well as other parties in Hong Kong. Those can rings were also brought to an NGO in Bangkok to make prosthesis for the needy.

    在2016年5月尾，我們的管理團隊舉辦了一個前往泰國曼谷的遊學團。「身體力行地貢獻社會」，是此行的一大重點。
    參加者在前往曼谷前，在宿舍社區中舉辦了玩具收集行動／籌款活動，之後將收集到的玩具及款項帶到位於曼谷的一所孤兒院。
    此外，我們亦成功地從宿舍社區中以及香港的其他組織中收集了很大量的汽水罐拉環，並將拉環送抵當地的一個非政府組織，幫助有需要人士製作義肢。

Scholarships


Quota: 10 / academic year (Maximum)

Amount equivalent to hall fee payable for one semester for double-room occupancy.
A cash award of HK$5,000 to HK$10,000 each.

Scholarships for Excellence and Amiable Living (SEAL)

Quota: 4-6 / academic year

The maximum amount for each scholarship shall not exceed the amount of the hall fee payable for one semester for double-room occupancy.


We will consider your:

Leadership
for exemplary leadership skills and qualities such as interpersonal skills, event management skills, problem solving and organizational abilities in promoting hall life;

Dedication
for a positive and constructive attitude with proven records in contributing to the residential community in an on-going manner;

Innovation
for implementing innovative and creative activities to promote community service or multi-cultural awareness in the hall community;

Community Service
for contribution of excellent performance in a minimum of 25 hours of volunteer service organized by Chan Sui Kau Hall with excellent performance.


Interested residents should download the updated application form from the official website of the Student Residence Office.

Cornwall School

Our residents offer a year-long regular service targeting mentally-handicapped children in Cornwall School. Each volunteer must attend a mandatory training session before they can join the year-long regular service.

Volunteer Work in Bangkok

In May 2016, our Management Team organized a study tour to visit Bangkok, Thailand. Social contribution is one of our highlights in this tour. Before the trip, participants organized toys collection campaign / fund rasing in the hall community, which we were able to take those toys and money to an orphanage in Bangkok later in the tour. Furthermore, we also successfully collected tons of can rings from the hall community as well as other parties in Hong Kong. Those can rings were also brought to an NGO in Bangkok to make prosthesis for the needy.



曼谷義工之旅

在2016年5月尾，我們的管理團隊舉辦了一個前往泰國曼谷的遊學團。「身體力行地貢獻社會」，是此行的一大重點。參加者在前往曼谷前，在宿舍社區中舉辦了玩具收集行動／籌款活動，之後將收集到的玩具及款項帶到位於曼谷的一所孤兒院。此外，我們亦成功地從宿舍社區中以及香港的其他組織中收集了很大量的汽水罐拉環，並將拉環送抵當地的一個非政府組織，幫助有需要人士製作義肢。

Dr. Chow Oi Wah

周愛華女士 (Esther)





Hi, my name is Esther Chow, Residence Master of Chan Sui Kau Hall (Hall 5).

Congratulations to our freshmen as you begin your university journey with us. Living in the residence is significant part of university life experience, which has great impact on both personal and professional growth and development.

Being professionally trained in social work, and have worked in educational, health, social and community services contexts both in Hong Kong and Toronto, Canada, I enjoy meeting and working with people from all walks of lives. My passion in working with young people continuous to grow after joining CityU, and have the privilege in working with university students to develop future leaders, foster student mentoring activities and out-of-classroom discoveries across different disciplines locally and internationally.

Together with our residence tutors and residence association, our challenge are to bring out the best of our residents, nurture our strengths and core values, mobilize available resources, create opportunities, and work collaboratively with hopes and to make our society a better place for all.

CSK Hall is a warm and playful place, a second home away from home, where we cultivate confidence, trust, mutual respect, sense of belongings, and nurture career aspirations and lifelong friendships in this hall community. Welcome to visit H5, and come to meet with our local, non-local, international and sport talents…

We look forward to exploring how our experience would make a contribution to your personal and professional development in the coming years. Thank you, and see you around.

Esther



大家好，我是五號陳瑞球堂的舍監Esther。恭喜各位新生和我們一起開展你們的大學旅程。住宿舍是大學生活中非常重要的一部分，無論是對於個人還是專業上的成長和發展都有巨大的影響。

首先簡單介紹一下我自己，由於曾經在社工專業上受過專業的訓練，以及在香港和加拿大多倫多兩地均有從事教育，健康，社會及社區服務等各方面的經歷，我十分享受和社會上不同階層的人接觸和合作。而我對於和年輕人一起合作的熱情在進入城大后更是越來越高，尤其在和大學生合作去培育未來的社會領導，促進學生輔導員活動還有本地甚至國際間的跨學科課外探索活動幾方面格外有興趣。

我們的宿舍導師和宿生會積極培養學生的才能和灌輸他們核心價值，動員一切可利用資源，製造機會和希望，共同合作，令社會變得更美好。

陳瑞球堂是一個溫暖而快樂的地方，是我們離開家庭后的第二個家。在這個小社區里我們逐漸建立自信心，信任，互相尊重和歸屬感，培養我們未來職業生涯上的抱負和建立一生長存的友誼。歡迎各位常來探訪Hall 5， 和我們的本地生，國際學生以及運動精英們多多交流。

我們期待在來年看到宿舍經歷對你們個人和專業上的成長。謝謝大家，再見。

Esther
